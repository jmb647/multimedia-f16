Climate Change Awareness

What is Climate Change?

Below are the four major effects that climate change has had on our Earth so far

Extreme Weather

Our Earth has been facing extreme changes in weather as it continues to warm due to the release of too many greenhouse gases in the air.

Sea Level Rise

As the Earth continues to warm, arctic areas are melting creating an overabundance of water which will cause the sea level to rise.

Fossil Fuels

Due to massive amounts of pollution and the use of fossil fuels, the sun's energy is being trapped in the atmosphere and continues to warm our Earth.

Temperature Rise

The Earth has been facing warmer weather due to the amount of greenhouse gases that are being exposed to Earth's atmosphere.
